# 1

$$
\begin{alignat}{2}
{} - AC(D,B) + A(C,B)D - C(D,A)B + (C,A)DB  &= &{} - \cancel{ACDB} - \cancel{ACBD} \\
                                            &  &{} + \cancel{ACBD} + ABCD          \\
                                            &  &{} - CDAB          - \cancel{CADB} \\
                                            &  &{} + \cancel{CADB} + \cancel{ACDB} \\\\\\
                                            &= &{} ABCD - CDAB
\end{alignat}
$$
